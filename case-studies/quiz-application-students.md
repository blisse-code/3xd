# Quiz Application for Students

**Year:** 2021  
**Type:** Product Design  
**Domain:** EdTech  
**Role:** UX Designer / Researcher

---

## Context and Constraints

This project targeted students from low-income families using self-paced learning tools. The platform needed to work on low-end Android devices with intermittent connectivity. Budget and timeline were tight.

**Key constraints:**
- Users had limited digital literacy and inconsistent internet access
- Devices ranged from budget Android phones to shared classroom tablets
- The content team operated with minimal design support

1. **Gamified quiz flow** with progress bars, streaks, and rewards
2. **Simplified linear flow** with reduced UI elements and larger touch targets
