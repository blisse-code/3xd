# Hotel Booking Experience

**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Product Design  
**Domain:** Travel  
**Role:** UX Designer

---

## Context and Constraints

Redesigning the search and booking flow for a hotel platform. Travellers were abandoning the funnel during preference selection, indicating a mismatch between what the system offered and what users needed to make a decision.

## The Real Problem

The filtering system exposed too many options with poor hierarchy. Users could not distinguish between "nice to have" and "deal breaker" preferences, leading to decision paralysis.

## Options Considered

1. **Progressive disclosure** of filters based on search context
2. **Preference-weighted ranking** that surfaced best matches first
3. **Comparison view** allowing side-by-side evaluation of shortlisted hotels
